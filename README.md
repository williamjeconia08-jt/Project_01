# SmartCent

> Empowering teenagers to build lifelong financial confidence through interactive learning and real-world simulations.

---

## 📖 About SmartCent

SmartCent is a financial literacy platform designed for teenagers aged **13–16**. Instead of relying on traditional lessons and textbooks, SmartCent teaches money management through interactive simulations, quizzes, gamified challenges, and realistic financial scenarios.

The platform encourages users to **learn by doing**, allowing them to practice financial decision-making in a safe virtual environment before applying those skills in real life.

---

## 🎯 Mission

To make financial education engaging, practical, and accessible by providing teenagers with a safe environment to develop healthy financial habits through interactive experiences.

---

## 🌍 The Problem

Many teenagers leave school with little understanding of how money works.

Essential topics such as budgeting, saving, banking, investing, taxes, and financial planning are often introduced too late—or not at all. As a result, many young adults begin managing real money without the confidence or practical experience needed to make informed financial decisions.

Traditional financial education often relies on reading and memorization rather than practical application.

SmartCent aims to change that.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

SmartCent combines education with simulation.

Rather than simply explaining financial concepts, the platform allows users to:

- Learn financial concepts through interactive lessons
- Practice managing virtual money
- Complete quizzes and challenges
- Make financial decisions in realistic scenarios
- Track their learning progress
- Build confidence before managing real finances

---

## ⭐ Core Principles

Every feature in SmartCent follows these guiding principles:

- **Learn by Doing** – Practical experience is more effective than memorization.
- **Safe Experimentation** – Users can make mistakes without real-world consequences.
- **Gamified Learning** – Learning should be engaging, rewarding, and enjoyable.
- **Real-World Relevance** – Every lesson should prepare users for actual financial situations.
- **Continuous Growth** – Financial education is a lifelong journey.

---

## 🚀 Minimum Viable Product (MVP)

The first version of SmartCent focuses on delivering a complete and polished learning experience with the following features:

- User authentication
- User profile
- Dashboard
- Virtual wallet
- Budgeting module
- Quiz engine
- XP and level system
- Achievement badges
- Progress tracking

Features such as investing, taxes, AI coaching, parent dashboards, teacher dashboards, and advanced simulations are planned for future releases.
